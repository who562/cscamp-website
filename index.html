About Us

CSCAMP is a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity for interested and talented students to develop their Computer Science skills over the summer, with expert faculty guiding and training them through national and international competitions in a fun and challenging atmosphere. It is a one-day camp with intense seminars, practice sessions, USACO mock tests, and enjoyable activities.
